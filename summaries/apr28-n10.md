## WHAT WAS LEARNED

The direct product theorem (if C1 ⊆ F_3^m and C2 ⊆ F_3^k are cap sets, then C1×C2 ⊆ F_3^(m+k) is a cap set) is the strongest tool available here, yielding 2025 via the Pellegrino-45 cap product. The greedy ceiling in F_3^10 is approximately 1132 (weight-ordered) and no local search approach could exceed it without the product structure, confirming that the greedy local optimum is a deep basin very far from 2474. The Edel-112 cap in F_3^6 (which would enable a 112×20=2240 product) could not be found computationally via SA within the 5-minute budget — SA consistently plateaus around 80-82 out of 112, suggesting the Edel cap has a specific algebraic structure (related to the Hill cap in PG(5,3)) that is not accessible via random search. The gap between the best achieved (2025) and the literature lower bound (2474) corresponds precisely to the gap between the achievable product constructions and the Edel algebraic construction, which requires explicit knowledge of the Hill/Edel cap coordinates that could not be reconstructed computationally here.
